AI-Powered SOP Capture for Real Restaurant Operations
What It Is

Scribe is an AI-based process documentation tool that records real work as it happens and automatically converts it into clear, step-by-step SOPs and checklists.

Instead of asking managers to write procedures from memory, Scribe captures actual execution—clicks, actions, sequences—and turns them into standardized documentation.

No theory. No guessing. No “this is how I think it works.”

Why It Works in Restaurants

Restaurants don’t fail because people don’t care.
They fail because critical knowledge lives in people’s heads.

Scribe removes:

Memory dependency

Personality-based execution

“Ask Sarah how to do it” systems

And replaces them with:

Transferable procedures

Checklist-first execution

Repeatability across shifts and locations

This makes it ideal for high-turnover, high-pressure environments.

What Scribe Does (In Plain Terms)
1. Captures Real Workflows

A manager or lead performs a task once:

Opening procedures

Cash handling

Inventory counts

Ordering

Closing duties

Manager audits

Scribe records the steps exactly as performed.

No rewriting. No summarizing.

2. Auto-Creates SOPs

Scribe converts that workflow into:

Step-by-step instructions

Clean checklists

Visual references (when needed)

This becomes the official process, not someone’s interpretation.

3. Standardizes Without Overwriting Reality

Unlike traditional SOP writing:

It doesn’t “optimize” prematurely

It documents what actually works today

You can refine later — but first you lock consistency.

Where Scribe Fits in a Scalable SOP System

Scribe is not your whole system.
It is your capture engine.

Best Uses

Opening & closing procedures

Cash handling SOPs

New-location documentation

What It Should NOT Replace

Accountability

Manager verification

Posted checklists
