# Rebrand: Remove All AI References — Position as Operator-Built Systems Platform

The app currently brands itself around AI ("Restaurant AI Consulting," "AI-powered," etc.). We need to remove ALL references to AI throughout the entire codebase and rebrand around operator expertise and systems. The value is the frameworks and methodology — not the technology delivering it.

**Core positioning shift:**
- OLD: "AI-powered restaurant operations consulting platform"
- NEW: "Restaurant operations systems built by operators, for operators"

Work through each section below carefully. Search the entire codebase for each term to make sure nothing is missed.

---

**Find and replace across the entire project:**

### index.html
- Page title: Change `<title>Restaurant AI Consulting</title>` → `<title>Restaurant Operations Consulting</title>` (or whatever the new brand name is — see options below)
- Meta description: Change `"AI-powered restaurant operations consulting platform. Replace chaos with systems."` → `"Restaurant operations consulting platform. Replace chaos with systems."`
- `apple-mobile-web-app-title`: Update to match new name

- **Restaurant Operations Consulting** (straightforward)

## Summary of the Philosophy

The AI is the engine under the hood. Users should never think about it. They should feel like they're accessing a **consulting platform built by someone who's lived the restaurant life** — not a chatbot. Every piece of copy should answer: "Why should I trust this?" with "Because it was built by operators who've been through it" — NOT "Because AI."

Think of it like this:
- Grammarly doesn't say "AI fixes your grammar" — it says "Write with confidence"
- Uber doesn't say "AI matches you with drivers" — it says "Tap a button, get a ride"
- This app should say "Get the right staffing plan for tonight" — not "AI generates your staffing plan"
